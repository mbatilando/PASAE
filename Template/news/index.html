PASAE provides academic support through peer counseling, class advising, and various workshops. Our members come from a wide variety of fields in engineering and science as well as architecture, and represent both lower and upperclassmen. This creates a friendly and knowledgeable environment in which to seek advice and guidance. We offer TeleBEARS class advising for both Fall and Spring semesters, study nights with refreshments, workshops in office productivity software, website design, financial planning, and plenty of opportunities to network with alumni.
